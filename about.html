Our Story

Founded a century ago, Grand Plus College has remained steadfast in its commitment to academic integrity and inclusive community. We believe that diversity of thought and background is the cornerstone of a world-class education.

What started as a small teaching institute has evolved into a leading research university, pioneering new methodologies in technology, social sciences, and the arts. Our values are built on the bedrock of transparency, innovation, and social responsibility.
